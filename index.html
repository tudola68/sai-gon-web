Saigon city

Saigon welcomes children from all over the country who come here to study, work and live. The noise, the scene of busy traffic, the scene of traffic jams often make newcomers feel suffocated and frustrated. When gradually getting used to the city, people can comfortably go on crowded roads, follow each line of cars through junctions and intersections, and then turn somewhere in small alleys. Saigon Street has many turns, like the cheap turns of fate that brought people here. Also, someone who is a stranger becomes infatuated with this place, as familiar as his own soul, loves Saigon every morning with his friends, coffee in the morning, it doesn't matter if it's coffee, it's simply an excuse to gather. practice talking. It is the feeling of delight in watching the city wake up, radiant, stretching proudly in the morning sun. Every afternoon, we invite each other to roadside restaurants with popular dishes, and are full of colorful stories.
